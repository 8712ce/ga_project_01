David Sherman Milton Jr. is a graphic artist and aspiring software engineer. His hand-painted signage and murals live in Trader Joe’s grocery stores, and many independent businesses (from breweries to yoga studios) throughout Southern California. He is currently working on an epic, dark fantasy graphic novel, Cleo & Bjørn.

In college, David started building basic websites to display his comics and other artwork. But with the rise of web-based/web-hosted software, he eventually stopped WYSIWYG-ing his own sites. However, his love for tinkering with CSS and problem solving never waned, and he is currently transitioning into a professional career in web development. He is eager to grow as a developer with a competitive team and to ultimately create and implement fun, whimsical, story-based, experiences for (mobile) app users.

David spends all of his free time studying programming languages, painting, building bicycles and furniture, camping in the middle of nowhere with his 2003 Toyota Tacoma, listening to NPR podcasts, and finding stretches of road to get his Triumph into 5th gear.
